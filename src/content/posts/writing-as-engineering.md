Engineering organizations run on decisions. Decisions run on shared context. Writing is how you scale context without scaling meetings.

## What good internal writing does

- Captures **intent**, not only outcome
- Survives team churn
- Creates a paper trail for tradeoffs

## What to write down early

Architecture decision records do not need to be long. A one-page ADR with context, decision, and consequences is enough to prevent repeated debates.

## For individuals

Publishing essays—even infrequently—sharpens thinking. The public mirror forces you to name assumptions and admit uncertainty.

---

Writing is not separate from building. It is how you debug your own understanding.
